Key pricing insights for advisors

High margin categories

Long-sleeve polo (15% avg discount), coat (25%), trousers (30%). Push these first — better margin for the store.

Watch your discounts

Cardigan (44%), sports trousers (41%), T-shirt (40%) are heavily discounted. Avoid going deeper — it affects your bonus score.

Premium ticket target

Aim for ₽15,000+ per item when possible. Coat (₽45K), vest (₽17K), jacket (₽27K) are your premium anchors.
